\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Concrete strategy implementation for caring for cactus plants. 

\doxylink{class_cactus_care}{Cactus\+Care} implements the Strategy pattern to provide specific care instructions for cactus plants. Cacti require minimal watering, maximum sunlight, and well-\/draining soil.
