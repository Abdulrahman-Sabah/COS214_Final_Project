Detailed Description

Concrete implementation of Plant for cactus plants.

The Cactus class represents a specific type of plant with low water requirements and high heat tolerance. It implements the Prototype pattern allowing for cloning of cactus instances. Cacti typically require minimal care and are well-suited for dry climates.
